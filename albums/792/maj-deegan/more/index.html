    This album was partly inspired by a recent visit to New York in which I took the Maj Deegan Expressway North, leaving behind the Bronx to meet a new family member Up State.

Also, thanks to the Kupillas & Heaney Family for endless support, the Sites Family for their monetary contribution, and of course my friends who don't know how not to support each other, especially Hunter Donaldson, Claire D'Aubin, Shawn O'Connor, Matt Martens, Paul "Sunshine" Sanders II, Joel Paul, Jared Hennessey, Joe Alexander.
