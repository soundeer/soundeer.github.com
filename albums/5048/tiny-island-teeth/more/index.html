    Reviews for Tiny Island Teeth

    by Simone Morelli


     Back to album

    Tiny Island Teeth was written and recorded in our Brooklyn apartment during the summer of 2011. Cakes and Tapes is responsible for the physical release- a limited edition of 50 cassette tapes. The album artwork was a collaboration between photographer Lily Roden (age 3) and Cory Siegler. Lily was given a handful of disposable cameras, and we ended up creating five different covers using her photos.

    An accompanying cassette was self-released simultaneously with Tiny Island Teeth, titled Tooth Notes. Tooth Notes is a collection of demos for Tiny Island Teeth, recorded with one microphone straight to tape. You can listen to that here:

    released 06 September 2011 Tiny Island Teeth was recorded with a handful of our favorite artists and friends. Among them are: Charlotte Oleena - vocals on "Swell" Ellen Johansing - vocals on "Pinch" and "Into The Sea" Marie Clerel - vocals on "Tiny Island Teeth" Jordan Lee - vocals on "Runoff" Matt Dallow - glockenspiel and saw on "Swell" and accordion on "I Want To Go Home" Here is a list of links of our friends credited: Sea Oleena:
