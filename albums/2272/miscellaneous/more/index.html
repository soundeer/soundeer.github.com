    A few other recordings that we have done for various things!

    1. Mackerel Jason (Acoustic)

    Robin did an acoustic version of this track off the EP: 'Rammin' It Home'.

    Buy here for however much you want or download for free by signing up to our mailing list at:

    1. Secure (Kerbdog Cover)

    Recorded for a Kerbdog tribute album on Stressed Sumo Records. Compilation also features Frank Turner (Million Dead), Jamie Lenman (Reuben) and Dave McPherson (InMe).

    Available from iTunes or on CD from

    1. Freak (Live Silverchair Cover)

    Recorded by Ioana Barbu for a Demon FM live session whilst on tour in September 2010.

    1. Don't Call it a Backcomb

    We rerecorded this track when we recorded Mackerel Jason for the first time but decided afterwards not to use it. Appeared on a Christmas card CD given out at our Westy Christmas show in 2009. Only a very rough mix.

    1. Welcome To Obscurity (Radio Edit)

    A slightly shorter version of the song that we used for radio plugging.
