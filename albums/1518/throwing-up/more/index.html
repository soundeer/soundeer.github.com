    Reviews for Throwing Up

    by BEST FRIENDS


     Back to album

    'Throwing Up' EP released on Art Is Hard Records March 2012

    "For this release Best Friends have put together a 21cm square booklet of halloween parties, crowdsurfing at house shows and other rad photos. The CD is encased within the booklet itself. At the risk of sounding terribly middle class, its a bit like one of those coffee table books - will hopefully get some pictures up soon!"
