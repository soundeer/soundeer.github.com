    Reviews for Generation Y Not

    by KYNAN


     Back to album

    Generation Y Not is a compilation of 7 young Los Angeles artists, all of whom are part of the enormous movement of youth in music. The compilation is a feature on the paramount shift in music culture, how because of the internet and the way this generation has grown up with the ability to create and share so easily, music has been taken into the hands of youth.

    In order to introduce you to the artists of Generation Y Not, 7 videos have been made, one with each musician, three of which have been posted on PORTALS in the past few weeks, and four of which will be posted on PORTALS throughout the coming weeks.

    JAXART Records will be pressing 1,000 CD’s that will be distributed and available for free. More details coming soon.

    released 03 April 2012
