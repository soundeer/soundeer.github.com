    Reviews for We Are The Captivated

    by Van Atta High


     Back to album

    We Are The Captivated was the band's first release after being together for only a few months. The band consisted of Stephen Kellner, Socrates Moutsakis, Santino LoConte, Kyle Brazier, and Jarret Gallipoli (RIP <3).

    All of the band members were 14-16 during the time of recording.

    released 26 September 2006 Drums by Santino LoConte Guitar by Socrates Moutsakis and Kyle Brazier Bass by Jarret Gallipoli (RIP <3) Vocals by Stephen Kellner Produced by Ethan Bill and Stephen Jasko. Recorded, Mixed, and Mastered by Ethan Bill at Room 368 Studios
