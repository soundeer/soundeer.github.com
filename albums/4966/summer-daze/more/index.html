    Reviews for Summer Daze

    by International Affairs


     Back to album

    We tried to capture the feelings that summer represents-reminiscent of one's youth; happiness, romance, heartbreak, possibility, and excitement. Yet, with an underlying sadness because deep down you knew it would all soon be over.

Special thanks to Mark Troth Lewis, Joshua Griffler, Daniel Plenge and Johnny Green. This E.P. is dedicated to the loving memory of Don Hill.
