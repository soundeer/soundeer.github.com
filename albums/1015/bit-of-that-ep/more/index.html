    Reviews for Bit Of That EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Campbell & Detnum, two talented guys from Australia who share a passion for jackin house and booming basses. What can we say, there’s some amazing talent in Australia that we just keep bumping into. ‘Bit Of That’ appeared first on a mixtape by Sharkslayer and now it’s time to unleash the whole package. Designed to the dance floor, this ep will keep them feet stomping and make you jack. Don’t miss out on some essential house tunes friend. Also check the bonus cut, ‘Tonight’ and try those basses at the club, whoomp.

    released 30 December 2011
