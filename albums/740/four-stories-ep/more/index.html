    Four track EP, 2011. Released by Calico Cat. CAL002

    released 12 February 2012 All songs were written by Philip J. Sutton except Plane in the Sky, which was written by Jason Corace. Copyright control. Recorded and mixed by Gary Olson at Marlborough Farms, Summer 2010. The Soft City on this recording are Dora Lubin: vocals, Jason Corace: rhythm and lead guitars, Turner Stough: bass, Kyle Forester: keyboards and lead guitars, and Philip J. Sutton: drums and guitar.
