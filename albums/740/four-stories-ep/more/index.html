    Four track EP, 2011. Released by Calico Cat. CAL002

Recorded and mixed by Gary Olson at Marlborough Farms, Summer 2010. The Soft City on this recording are Dora Lubin: vocals, Jason Corace: rhythm and lead guitars, Turner Stough: bass, Kyle Forester: keyboards and lead guitars, and Philip J. Sutton: drums and guitar.
