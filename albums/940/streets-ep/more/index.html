    Reviews for Streets EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Ready for some bangers? We got you.

    Enter Chauffeur aka Thom Theroux from Toronto, Canada. Thom is our man who can make crazy party tunes that will twist your ears and at the same time have that pop sensibility we love in dance music. This is Chauffeurs debut ep, so listen closely.

    This is a 5-track ep, with tracks that range from twisted disco to speaker bruising rave to bass heavy pop. Fresh in a way when we first heard a genre called ‘electro’. Dark and grimey like the alleys of major cities, the unexpected lurking around the corner.

    released 01 July 2010 Written and produced by Chauffeur
