    by Shortcake Collage Tape

    Video for "A Wrong Vacation"

    夏への扉を叩くビート。色あせたループ。

    Shortcake Collage Tape（略称ショコラテ）の正式リリース第一号となる本作は、過去のデモやDaydrop時代の音源とは打って変わり、

    60年代ソフト・ロックに根ざしながらも「チルウェーブ以後」を強く感じさせるサウンドに仕上がっている。

    ジャンク感いっぱいのサンプルの渦に巻き込まれて、

    第三世界のサーフ・シティへ……。

    Beats that knock on the door into summer.

    Loops that are thoroughly faded.

    Unlike Azusa Suga's previous work as Daydrop, this EP, which is his very first official release as Shortcake Collage Tape,

    is rooted in '60s sunshine pop but has a strong post-chillwave feeling.

    Get sucked into this whirlpool of junk samples,

    and take a trip to a third world surf city...

    Released on 5/2/2012
