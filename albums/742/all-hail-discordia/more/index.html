    Reviews for All Hail Discordia

    by By Divine Right


     Back to album

    released 18 November 1997 Originally released by Squirtgun Records. Remastered & reissued by Nettwerk Records. Recorded, mixed & produced by Brenndan McGuire at a cottage near Gravenhurst, Ontario. Additional overdubs on 4 track in a basement near you. Mixed at Electro-Magnetic Sound, Toronto. Jose Contreras sang, played guitar, keys, percussion & a little bass. Mark Goldstein played drums, percussion & keys. Cam Bull played bass and a little acoustic. All songs by Jose Miguel Contreras except "I Love Everybody" by Contreras, Goldstein and McGuire. "Highway Alien Paranoia" by Contreras & McGuire. "Fearless" by Contreras & Goldstein. Published by I Love Music (SOCAN). Mastered by Greg Calbi at Masterdisk, N.Y. Cover by Helios -
