    Reviews for Dozens EP

    by Dozens


     Back to album

    Press Quotes:

    "Beginning with a brilliant and teeming seascape—one of the most effective I’ve heard grafted onto a rock song...anyone already departing for land might regret not sticking around for side B...it’s a sound the Dozens do very, very well." - Coke Machine Glow

    "Sometimes atmospheric, sometimes disarming, Dozens EP demands a listen." - The Total Scene

    "This bizarrely bold and varied 20 minute sit broke past yesterday's early speed dating by 11am to pretty much own my afternoon...Dozens EP is a curve ball, throwing harmonies against swift talk-verses and the occasional scream, a traditional trio of instruments against organ and brass...and you guessed it, sand against space." - Stadiums and Shrines

    "The 5 tracks on this self-titled EP contain elements from almost every imaginable musical genre, from classical to ambient to jazz to soul to rock and points in between. They are held together with tight drum chops and are channeled through unorthodox arrangements and buoyed by dynamic musicianship and quavering falsetto vocals, boosting the emotional intensity...the band seem headed in the right direction and, when the mood strikes you to throw on something completely different, this 5-song EP is worth checking out." - Delusions of Adequacy
