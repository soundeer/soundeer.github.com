    Reviews for Balloons EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Top Billin welcomes Them Jeans to the Top Billin knighthood.

    If you know anything about the US dj scene, you have heard about Them Jeans for sure and if you’ve ever seen him, you will remember him (his the tallest dj we’ve ever seen with his 6 feet 8 inches figure). Jason aka Them Jeans has been running the infamous Dim Mak nights in Los Angeles for over 5 years now as a dj and promoter and makes excellent techy house tunes with big bass sounds. He’s had releases before on labels like Emynd’s CFB and Turntable Lab, he deejays like the devil and is one of the dopest dudes we’ve ever met.

    His first ep on Top Billin is a funky-styled house banger that has a lead weird enough to make you go wow at the club. Remixes come from fellow LA boys Camo Ufos (Mike B & Nate Day), Mad Decent’s own (and Diplo’s studio engineer) DJA and the next big thing in tropical beats, LOL Boys (don’t let that name fool you, these dudes are real).

    “I love this ep!” - SINDEN

    released 16 July 2010
