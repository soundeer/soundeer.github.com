    Reviews for Scrap Junts Get Gnard

    by jib kidder


     Back to album

    Scrap Junts Get Gnard OOP Gnar Tape available for download for the first time. This tape was / this download is / a collection of JK detritus starting with the First JK Track Ever (not counting a certain kind of completely-appropriate revisionism since no one else is in this band, which does not mean you can call me "Jib") and then descending to even worse and more uneven things like a cover of Porno for Pyros "Pets" and a little fucking around on a drum machine rigorously micro-tuned into a fake Gamelan 808, some vinyl destruction memoirs, derivative & inauthentic NOLA Bounce, "calliope" (the first JK cut-up video), the Breeders "iris" (<3 U), oh yeah some excellent vox by Josie and some metal-ruler guitar shredding too. Its basically a document of the death of my PC Desktop, an unruly beast I clung to like an abusive parent with an unusually warm body temperature from Y2K-2008. Experience my nostalgia for me!

    released 11 November 2009 SSC: computer, technics 1200, guitar, objects, field recordings, mandolin, mechanical toys
