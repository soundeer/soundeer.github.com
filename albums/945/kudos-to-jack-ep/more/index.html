    Reviews for Kudos To Jack EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Top Billin welcomes Fex Fellini, a great German who makes excellent house with a tech twist, with added world music elements. If you like the stuff from Solo, Zombie Disco Squad, Renaissance Man or Camel, and If you liked the Joyce Muniz & Shanti Roots – ‘Get Your Conga On’ ep earlier this year on Top Billin, you’re gonna love Fex’s debut.

    Remixes are also in the works and coming soon from Them Jeans, Desto, Mvsevm and LaBerge, so be on the lookout for those as well. All hail the FEXICAN.

    Because we ain’t all about crazy bangers you know.

    released 10 September 2010 Written and produced by Fex Fellini
