    *Dedicated to Wulfstan, Bishop Of Worcester (1002 - 1016)*

    WULFSTAN continues where BEAK>'s debut album left off.... live in a studio room with no overdubs.

    "Wulfstan" is accompanied by 3 live tracks including "Ham Green", "Blagdon Lake" plus a live version of the title track.

    Also available on 12" vinyl.... Limited pressing of 500 copies only.

    BEAK> are Geoff Barrow > Billy Fuller > Matt Williams

    released 17 April 2010 Barrow/ Fuller/ Williams
