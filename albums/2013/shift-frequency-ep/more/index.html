    Reviews for Shift Frequency Ep

    by Cosa Nostra


     Back to album

    Hailing from Makati City, Philippines, SimilarObjects

    is part of an integral group of beatmakers currently pushing the envelope in the country.

    Inspired by Carl Jung's Theory on collective consciousness and the relationship between cosmic entities, he treats each of his beats like a work of art where elements are selected carefully so that they may work together harmoniously, wherein each beat serves a specific purpose, be it for himself or the listener.

    "similarobject-within-similarobject"

    We are extremely delighted to be putting out his first Ep on the label, entitled 'Shift Frequency', and we invite you to take some time out (approximately 16 mins out of your daily routine) and embark on this cosmic journey.

    Also, a big shout out to Tof Zapanta for the incredible artwork.

    released 09 June 2012 All tracks written, produced & mastered by Jorge Wienekev
