    Reviews for A New Life

    by Franck Kartell


     Back to album

    RM 004

    Musically A New Life is influenced by early Human League and Soft Cell but don't sound like them. The schemes don't help the healing process. Analog synths meet digital ones creating a sound which is deep rooted into the 80s. Post punk and new wave are the main elements but if you think about minimal, well, the sound isn't that minimal if you think that the songs have three or four melodic synth lines. Using a bit of analog dirtiness and warmth, A New Life want to gather cool sounds with catchy melodies. "A New Life", "Smile Again" and the instrumental "Grand Opening" are the first three tracks that an Italian guy called M.P. composed after breaking his daily routine at age 44. The process has started, please take a seat and enjoy.

    Art Cover By : Chris Vonsteiner

    released 07 October 2011
