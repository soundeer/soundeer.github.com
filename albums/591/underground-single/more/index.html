    Reviews for "Underground" Single

    by Orca Orca


     Back to album

    “Underground” is the first single off the next Orca Orca release entitled Make Live. It’s a redux-version of a demo that was released in November 2010 and has undergone a few changes in the live setting this year that I wanted to capture on record.

    Make Live will be released on a limited edition (100), hand painted cassette and will feature 6 new Orca Orca recordings.

    released 21 February 2012 Writen and recorded by Jim Hewitt. contact:

    Artwork by Jesse Treece.
