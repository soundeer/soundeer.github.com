    Reviews for Falling EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    One Dark Martian (ODM) has been making a name for himself on the UK House and Garage scene since around 2004. He has gained massive support from some of the scenes biggest DJ's and Producers including DJ EZ, Karl "Tuff Enuff" Brown, MJ Cole plus legendary producer and Daft Punk collaborator Todd Edwards. This new ep from him showcases his ability to create beautiful pop music without forgetting his roots on the Garage scene, the drums are crispy and there's plenty of bass to go around. ODM has a whole load of different musical delights up his sleeve that he will be sharing with you, so keep your eye and ears well n truly peeled for this true knight. We chose only one remixer for this great release, that being none other than New Jersey Garage legend Todd Edwards. Boom!

    released 11 June 2012
