    Reviews for Been Meaning to Tell You + 16 Hidden Bonus Remixes

    by Friends of Friends Music


     Back to album

    Been Meaning to Tell You is the 2nd album from Ernest Gonzales. The download includes 13 original tracks, plus 16 remixes, a digital art book of notes about each of the songs and 13 illustrations by international artists.

    The bonus Remix Album features remixes by Aether, Copy, CYNE, Daedelus, David Ramos, Faunts

    Faux Pas, Matthew David, Mexicans with Guns

    Mirm, Ocuban, Osos .o, Pollination, Take, Yppah, Zoon van Snook.

    The digital art book includes notes written by Ernest Gonzales about each song as well as illustrations by Martin Allais, Diego Chavez, Popenkov Denis, Mitxel Gonzalez, Marcel Kaczmarek, Shaun Koplow, Jacob Livengood, Aaron McKinney, Archan Nair, Andy Pitts, Ashkahn Shahparnia, Alexandre Szewinski, Jon Turner, and Alexis Ziritt.

    released 16 February 2010
