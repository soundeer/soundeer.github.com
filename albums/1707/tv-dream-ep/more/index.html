    Reviews for TV Dream EP

    by Friends of Friends Music


     Back to album

    Setting the tone for his upcoming LP due in August on Friends of Friends, Jasper Patterson aka Groundislava returns with the sun soaked TV Dream EP, a dynamic composition of luscious soundscapes drawn from 80s nostalgia and a Wedidit attitude.

    The five track composition revolves around the title track, featuring the enigmatic Clive Tanaka, in an ebullient ode to a friendship's inception drenched in summery vibes and ghostly playfulness. From the island breeze of "Weekend In The Tropics", and staggered slo-groove of "Reflecting", to the symphonic "Salt of Love" and "Tower Jump Suicide", Groundislava provides an eclectic spin and unique approach to electronic moods and timbres.

    released 26 June 2012
