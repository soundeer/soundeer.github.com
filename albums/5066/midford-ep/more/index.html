    Reviews for Midford (EP)

    by Hunting Club


     Back to album

    2 Song EP - Midford

    "Minneapolis indie-folkers are back with a new two track EP, Midford, a bridge between last year’s spectacular Plaid Album and their forthcoming sophomore LP. 'In a Villa' picks up right where the Plaid Album left off, showcasing the band’s ability to write catchy, yet unique folk influenced rock ‘n roll."

    -Erik Burg (One Thirty BPM)

    "Both tracks on this two-song release are gems that you’ll want to get your hands on, but the first track, entitled 'Bangkok', suggest a great growth in the band’s sound and dynamic. There are more layers, more atmosphere, better hooks, and their notable brilliant dynamic changes are far more brilliant than they ever have been before. Hunting Club creates the feel of chamber pop without the chamber orchestra, with the Steen brothers acting as precisely as an orchestra director would."

    -Banana Static

    released 23 August 2011 All Song written and performed by Hunting Club Hunting Club is Bob DuBois, Nate Dykstra, Eric Pasi, Kyle Steen, Justin Steen Recorded and mixed by Jeff Marcovis at Sound Gallery Studios Mastered by Rob Schlette at Anthem Mastering Album cover by Justin Steen
