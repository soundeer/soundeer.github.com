    Reviews for The Death Of Six By Seven Vinyl EP 2

    by six by seven


     Back to album

    2nd CD of Vinyl Cd's limited. Each Cd will have 4 tracks or more and be numbered and sent out according to the order they are bought in. (I don't keep numbers 1-5 for myself!)

    With these Vinyl CD's I want to try and capture the spirit of demo / Lo Fi recordings and won't be using any computers and record only to tape or portastudio. I love the sound of these type of recordings, they always capture something spontaneous that gets lost when the songs are 'properly' recorded. These Cd's are expensive to manufacture and so I have no choice but to sell them at £10.

    But rest assured, for those of you who will collect the cd's, you will be helping me to make my next album properly with a band and release it commercially and I will be offering you the chance to pick the songs you want on the album and giving you a credit on the album sleeve.

    This is an experiment! The music industry has changed so much that I want to try something different. This is a way of releasing the 'demos' of the album before the album is released and making you part of the recording process of the album. I hope it works!

    released 23 April 2012 released 12 March 2012 Recorded and mixed on Tascam portastudios, tape and digital. All tracks written and recorded by Chris Olley. Instruments played: Vocals, Martin Acoustic, harmonica, Rickenbacker 330, Vox AC 30, Fender Jazz bass, Hammond Organ, Logan String Machine, Moog Slim Phatty, Alesis SR-16 Drum Machine. Pedals: JamMan Solo Looper Pedal, Coopersonic Germaniac Fuzz, EH Worm, EH Poly Chorus, EH Small Stone, EH Small Clone, Tech 21 Bass sans amp.
