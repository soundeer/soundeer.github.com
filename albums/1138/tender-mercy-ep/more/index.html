    Reviews for Tender Mercy EP

    by Gross Magic


     Back to album

    Au Palais are a brother-sister twosome originally from Toronto and now located in London, they create beat-driven effereal Gothic Pop soliciting comparisons with M83, Zola Jesus and even Daft Punk's TRON: Legacy score.

    "Au Palais' forthcoming Tender Mercy EP, with its shimmering neon optimism and soaring vocals, finds a way to let in a bit more light but then there beneath it all is a rumbling low end, creating just the right amount of contrast and reminding you why these guys take care to note on their Bandcamp that they "record their music at night" - Pitchfork Best New Music

    Tracklisting:

    1. Tender Mercy

    2. Pathos

    3. Because The Night

    4. H.o.l.l.a.n.d

    released 05 December 2011
