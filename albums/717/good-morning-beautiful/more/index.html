    Reviews for Good Morning Beautiful

    by By Divine Right


     Back to album

    released 01 April 2001 Extended pre-release version. Limited to 500 copies on CD. "Buy CD" links to 12-track commercial release. "the spirit is the song of a heart without walls..." Produced by Jose Miguel Contreras and Joao de deus Carvalho with J. Andrew Magoffin. Recorded at the House of Miracles in London by J. Andrew Magoffin and at Umbrella Sound by Joao de deus Carvalho, December 1999 - November 2000. Mixed and mastered at Umbrella by Joao de deus Carvalho, August 2000 - March 2001. Additional recording and mixing at Umbrella by Greg O'Shea. Art and design by Tyler Clark Burke. Performed by Jason Ball, Joao de deus Carvalho, Jose Miguel Contreras, Cam Giroux, Colleen Hixenbaugh, Dylan Hudecki, J. Andrew Magoffin, Dave Merritt, Saidah Baba Talibah (courtesy of Blaxam). All music and lyrics by Jose, except "Hugger of Trees" (lyrics by Jose / John Broomfield); "Sweet Lovin'" (music by Jose / Spike Love); "Supernatural" (lyrics by Jose / Joao); "One More City" (music by Jose / Ron Levy / Jurij Konje); "Back to You" (music by Joao / Jose, lyrics by Joao / Jose / Gord Downie). Maximum respect to Monica Avendano, Joao, Andy, Spike, Ron, Jurij, Gord, Greg, Cam, Saidah, Melissa, Tyler, Peaches, Carlos E., the Child and K., Ferrara-Contreras mob, Jaimie Stanley, Dan Faux, Kurt Swinghammer, Brenndan McGuire, Jason Collett, 6 Draper (B, R & R), Roger Moutenot, John Hall, Andrew Rodriguez, Living Water Assembly (Jason, Paul, Matt), Lonnie James, Teenage USA, Dave Neufeld, Nick Holmes, Dan Burke, Skinny, Sandy, Colin Lewis, Dave Young, Jennifer Lorraine and the Hugger of Trees.
