    Reviews for Distro Nox EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Announcing some more great music, this time from our Australian brother, Adam Bozzetto. We had a blast with Adam last time in Sydney and ever since we started putting his ‘I Want To Be With Two’ on mixes, there has been such a demand for that tune so here it comes. Adam makes amazing high-powered disco/ house and works for Heaps Decent in Australia, great dude.

    "Adam Bozzetto has always been one to mix things up. From his early rotations of rave-heavy beats to his current influences of French and German synth-tech, Bozzetto lets his style of sound speak for itself. Now after more than six years of helming the decks, the Australian-bred DJ has launched his production career, sporting a vibe that is equally eclectic as his sets. Much like his tour schedule, Bozzetto prefers variety in both his track selection and production work. In defying the traditional genre borders of dance music, Bozzetto creates beats that are quick, think and always on the move."

    released 01 December 2010 Written and produced by Adam Bozzetto
