    Reviews for Nomeva EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Hailing from Austria, DJ Dizzy is a true music lover with his taste ranging from idm to tropical vibes, bass music and beyond. With his productions he creates a wide-range to body shaking tunes, not forgetting a certain sensitiveness that seems to be lacking in today's club music. Already getting support for his past productions from Drop The Lime, Toy Selectah, Malente, Wax Motif, wildlife!, Mad Decent, BBC Radio 1, Generation Bass and more, his brand new 'Nomeva' release will stay in your box all summer (and way longer). 'Nomeva' is an exploration to mystic chords, deep vocals, a drifty bassline and a percussive groove, creating a funky vibe at the dancefloor that totally embodies Dizzy's unique style. This being only Dizzy's second release ever, our boy is definitely going places. Remixes come from studio wizards Daniel Klauser, Roby Howler, Fast Feet and The Clonious.

    released 07 June 2012
