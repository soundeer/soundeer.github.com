    Reviews for inference (iii)

    by Fog Lake


     Back to album

    this is an album/ep i wrote and recorded entirely in the span of one night with dusty equipment. there was no real songwriting 'process'. although 80 percent of this album was created through the practice of stream-of-consciousness; I do feel the album has a prominent theme of loss, anxiety, time and love.

    all songs written, performed and produced by fog lake in the basement of his sad home. if anyone is interested in doing a cassette or cd release please contact me at 'hellofoglake@gmail.com'.

    released 03 June 2012
