    Reviews for Sparks - EP

    by Ferals


     Back to album

    We don't know if we like calling this an official EP--it's more of a demo. Ferals didn't start too long ago and sometimes we're not on the same page when we do stuff. These four tracks gave us some time to experiment and work with others, trying to find our own sound. We got to work with Sheena Truong who recorded vocals on Miles and the Modest Mouse cover, and Madison DeWalt, who sent in a vocal track for Talks. We also got to know some talented musicians along the way, and we'll get a chance to work with them in the near future. The production of our first 'album' will kick off soon, and it will definitely have a more unified sound. We might get around to re-recording these songs with new instruments and high-quality vocals. For now though, there's this. Let us know what you think of it.

    released 08 October 2011 Jan Colango, Madison DeWalt, Nach Dudsdeemaytha, and Sheena Truong.
