    Reviews for F - Energy Distortion

    by 7even Recordings


     Back to album

    F discovered Drum'n'Bass as a teenager. This passion pushed him to buy a pair of decks and some records. Slowly after he stepped in the production path, and began to produce Drum'n'Bass tracks on FL studio. He started progressively to show quite an interest for this emerging style of underground music called Dubstep, and eventually started producing Dubstep tracks in 2008. His growing love for Techno, and minimal forms of Dub led him to combine elements of both music with Dubstep.

    Travelling into different worlds, atmospheres and vibes, including classic unreleased tracks (0907, Poka, On The Corner, Confined System or Hologram) and brand new and unheard material (See The Light, Chillin’, Perspectives or Funk Injection), beautifully and loudly mastered by Shawn Joseph and Jacko Adams at Optimum Studios in Bristol, packed in an ultra fresh cover by Parisian designer David Lollia aka Synaptic, ‘Energy Distortion’ by F is a great album for music lovers as well as a heavy weapons' set for the DJs.
