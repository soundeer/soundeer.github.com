    'Klangverhältnisse records'

    Dear lovers of the magnetic tape underground,

    after some trouble with the pressing plant (seems as if this becomes a slapstick routine, ha!), I can finally announce the release of 'Sea Rejected' by Luxembourgian soundscapist BLUE DRESSED MAN on cassette tape. It's a limited c41 edition of 55. The album provides melancholy drones, all kinds of industrially seeming noises as well as the necessary portion of sadness and rage (LIYL VORTEX RIKERS, MOTION SICKNESS OF TIME TRAVEL, EXPO '70).

    Klangverhältnisse

    Glad to announce a new Klangverhältnisse release! The “Sea Rejected” album by Luxemburgian soundscapist BLUE DRESSED MAN will soon be published as KVH007. The limited cassette tape edition goes into production right now and will be available by the end of MMXI AD. Pre-orders accepted now!
