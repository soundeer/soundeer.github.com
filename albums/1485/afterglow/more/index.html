    Reviews for Afterglow

    by NanosauR


     Back to album

    This is my third album, 2nd full length. NanosauR presents Afterglow.

    "Okay so this is officially one of our favorite producers working in SF, didn't know anything about him before this week, but his album ($5 for 16 songs) is anything but typical electronica, this guy has a classic, almost daft punk, sensibility to his music, he is absolutely not trying to be someone else, which says a LOT in this world of copy and paste music. Check out Nanosaur on his FB and show some love by buying his album" - @Niteppl

    "The new album is full of sweet, sweet beats. Unfortunately, there are a lot more instrumental tracks this time around (what can I say? I love the dude’s voice!) but the album is HOT, and there are still a handful of tracks that Mike sings on." -

    "Afterglow is the latest album from FZB favorite NanosauR, aka ﻿Michael Solorzano. The 16 tracks continue Nanosaur's rise as a leader in the purveyance of chill music. The album is silky smooth, but with quirky samples showing Nanosaur's continued growth as a producer. Minimalist beats, airy synths and an abundance of textures layer together to make a compelling listening experience." -

    released 27 January 2012 Recorded, Mixed, and Mastered by Michael Solorzano. Fade To Gold Records 2012.
