    Reviews for Dateland

    by Ali Koehler


     Back to album

    The debut solo recordings of sweet and honest bedroom pop by Ali Koehler out on Neotomic Records!

    "Stepping out from behind the drum kit, Ali Koehler (Vivian Girls, Best Coast) shows off her skills as a singer, guitar/melodica player, and songwriter on this Neotomic Tape release. Stripped down bedroom pop with flourishes of her former bands but a voice of its own." - Permanent Records

    "Lo-fi, uber-cute indie-pop with light shades of punk from a lady with a really lovely voice... The whole album is just adorable." - Against the Odds

    released 15 March 2012
