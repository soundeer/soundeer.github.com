    Reviews for Daydream

    by Franck Kartell


     Back to album

    RM004

    Echö saw the day in october 11. Composed by 2 members, Mickaël Valli the french singer and Alexandre Portils Torner the catalan composer. Both met in Barcelona.

    After passionate conversations and joining their different universes, they decide to collaborate and create Echö.

    The first EP is about the plants/flowers how they can be mysterious and dark at least than just beautiful.

    Echö brings something fresh, poetic a bit naive like a child would do.

    Its definitely a sweet trip into a synth-wave with basic electronic shades.

    art work by Alexandra Vogt

    released 23 January 2012
