    Reviews for Forever The Feeling: A Believe In Sound Compilation

    by Believe In Sound


     Back to album

    'Forever The Feeling' is the very first Believe In Sound compilation. It features tracks from some of the blog's favourite artists and is free to download. The compilation's goal is to provide you with some feel-good electronic music.

    Major thanks to all the artists involved who were kind enough to contribute a track.

    Big thanks also to my buddies Feel Good Lost for the sublime artwork.
