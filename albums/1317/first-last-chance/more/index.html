    Reviews for First Last Chance

    by Roads and Boats


     Back to album

    "Named after a geekish god-game of donkeys, geese, and stone, Roads and Boats is the farthest thing from. And with our current obsession with tracks of light lyricism and heavy instrumentation, this Toronto-based, one-man musical extravaganza creates an atmosphere that’s magical, upbeat, and catchy. Though there isn’t much lyrically to absorb, Roads and Boats makes up in layer after melodious layer of euphonious euphoria.....Anything could happen. And anything has happened." - the mixtape files ( )

    released 01 December 2010 This is a loose collection of my early tracks that were released through various blogs starting in September 2010.
