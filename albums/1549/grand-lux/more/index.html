    by deadCAT


     Back to album

    "Tours is the moniker behind the Philadelphia-based musician, Dylan Sieh. Sieh dabbles his work into the subgenres of shoegaze and chillwave, orchestrating the sounds of sunny days and lazy layovers. His single "Grand Lux" provides songs that are perfect for long hot days in the shimmering summer sun."
