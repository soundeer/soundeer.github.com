    Reviews for Vertigo EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Holy bass music Batman! Even more great music from Australia! Blaze Tripp is a producer from Sydney, Australia who has made a name for himself by experimenting with heavy bass, addictive rhythms and jumping between genres and styles effortlessly. Blaze Tripp has an underground yet non-alienating style, which is an undeniably fresh take on club music. Always on the hustle / not one to sit still Blaze has released a handful of EP's in the last 12 months. These releases have found themselves on multiple compilations including Fabriclive 55: Drop The Lime, Ministry of Sound: Maximum Bass, Sneakerz Music Winter Sessions. Now watch out for his Vertigo EP coming on Top Billin with remixes from Hostage, Jaimie Fanatic & Ultravid. Big things are happening for Blaze, so don't be the last to hear about him & next time you hear about a our boy from Australia don't call the Fire Department, just prepare yourself for some dance floor fire. Welcome to Top Billin big Blaze!

    released 08 April 2011 Written and produced by Blaze Tripp
