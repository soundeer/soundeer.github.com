    Reviews for Gathered Ghosts

    by Gathered Ghosts


     Back to album

    Gathered Ghosts was done in my parent’s basement in Pullman, Washington using guitars, bass, Logic, my girlfriend’s autoharp, an ocarina that my parent’s brought back from Argentina, piano, some micro-Korg and a couple of other things.

    released 19 July 2011 Written, recorded and performed by Javier A. Suarez in Pullman, WA
