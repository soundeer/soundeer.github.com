    There really isn't much that compares to the music of I†† (eyedoublecross). Chris Hopper and Ultima Esperanza create film music devoid of images. The dark and deadly sounds of negative space. Something far closer to the godfathers of industrial than modern music, the core of their music lies in experimentation. There are brief sublime moments that break the profanity, but the result is a certain Lynch-ian confusion rather than respite. Their latest release "BSD" take listeners down a deep journey. Always macabre and challenging; it insists that you find a beauty within yourself, or perish in a nightmare of sonic brutality.
