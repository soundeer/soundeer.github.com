About Black Ice

by Brother/Ghost


 Back to album

Brother/Ghost are a band from Austin, TX. This CD was recorded sporadically from october '08 to july '09, by Sterling Dowdy and Jasper den Hartigh from Mt. Hope Revival Recordings. ( ) Brother/Ghost are: Sterling Dowdy-guitar, glock,piano, choir Jasper den Hartigh-guitar, vocals, glock Colby James-Bass, vocals Brandon Newton-drums. on this CD the drums were recorded by Austin Matherne, and bass for song 3 by Michael Delaney.
