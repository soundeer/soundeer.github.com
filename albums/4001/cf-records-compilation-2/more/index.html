    Reviews for CF Records Compilation 2

    by CF Records


     Back to album

    To celebrate the fifth anniversary of the first CF compilation, released exactly five years ago this week, we've put together our second compilation as a limited edition free download via bandcamp and soundcloud. Featuring greatest hits from all the CF releases so far by PETER'S HOUSE MUSIC, WHAT'S UP?, HIGH PLACES, LUCKY DRAGONS, GIRLS NAMES, NO MONSTER CLUB, CHARLES HURTS, CLOUD NOTHINGS, BOUNDARY JIM,SEA PINKS, MT. EERIE, NO KIDS, PFAEANS, HEAVY HAWAII, CHAMPAGNE DIAMOND/THE BRILLIANT LIGHT, JL SEAGULL, PATRICK KELLEHER, YOUNGER COLOURS and SCHOOL TOUR, it's our way of saying thanks to everyone who has bought a CF tape, record or CD since 2006 and we hope you'll be along (and bring your friends!) for the next five years of great music from more great bands. A big thanks also to all the artists on this comp for generously donating tracks, if you like what you hear please buy their music!

    CF, July 4th 2011
