    Reviews for HOLIDAYS - YOUNG LOVE [LP]

    by KIY RECORDS ®


     Back to album

    Every season needs a soundtrack. Think of sun-washed jeans and sandy dunes: summer is coming. And Holidays - Giaky, Ale, Luca and Simone - are ready to catch stars and sunshines and bottle it for every dream-pop come shoegaze fan. Their debut album "Young Love" (Lp available on KIY Records) is a reverb-drenched 10-track gem of surfy pop with influences ranging from Wild Nothing and Beach Fossils to Real Estate and The Drums. Just what you need to relax by the sea.

    released 05 March 2012
