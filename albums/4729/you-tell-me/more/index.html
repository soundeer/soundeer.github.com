    Reviews for You Tell Me

    by SPC ECO


     Back to album

    Recorded Mixed and Produced by Dean Garcia at the ELaB 2011

    YOU TELL ME is a new compilation album featuring 8 new previously unreleased songs and a best of selection of 4 tracks that were previously available from the 'Out Of The Sky' EP and the most recent 'Big Fat World' EP. All tracks have been recorded at ELaB studios in London featuring collaborations and guests from people we like to work with.
