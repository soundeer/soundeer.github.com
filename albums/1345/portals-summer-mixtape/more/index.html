    Reviews for PORTALS Summer Mixtape

    by PORTALS


     Back to album

    We at PORTALS are excited to be teaming up with the righteous bros at Chill Mega Chill Records to bring you our June installment of the Monthly Mixtape Series in a limited cassette run! We would like to extend and enormous thank you to all of the incredible track submissions... we are beyond confident that the PORTALS Summer Mixtape is the essential listening mix for all of your summer activities. And as always, support your favorite artists... Enjoy!
