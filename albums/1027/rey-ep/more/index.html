    Reviews for Rey EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Top Billin welcomes a Swedish brother to the family! Cristian Dinamarca is a close friend of Tony Senghore and the most exciting musical export to come from Sweden lately. With his always very chopped sample use and skill to make banging drums, CD is the DJ's favorite right now. Already getting support from all the big players, this release will stay in your box for a good time. Sweden is awesome.

    released 24 February 2012
