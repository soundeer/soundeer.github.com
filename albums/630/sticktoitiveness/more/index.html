    Reviews for "Sticktoitiveness"

    by The Super Friendz


     Back to album

    Remastered collection of most songs from "Mock Up, Scale Down" and five songs from "Play The Game, Not Games." Released in the United States only.

    released 23 May 1997 March Records MAR030 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 12, 13, 14, 16 & 17 produced by Brenndan McGuire and The Super Friendz and recorded in a house in the north-end of Halifax, December 1994. 7, 9, 10 & 11 recorded by Laurence Currie at Idea of East, Spring 1996. Drums recorded on 4-track by The Super Friendz. 15 recorded by Brenndan McGuire at Tiny Town, winter 1994. All songs by The Super Friendz The Super Friendz are: DREW YAMADA guitar and vocals CHARLES AUSTIN bass and vocals MATT MURPHY guitar and vocals LONNIE JAMES drums Drums on all tracks except 7, 9, 10, 11 & 15 by Dave Marsh Piano on 9 by Matt, 7 by Drew Design by Sheila Sachs B&W cover photo by Jannie McInnes All other photos by Catherine Stockhausen Thanks to: Family Yamada, Pete Baillie, Tim Brennan, Jannie McInnes, Jay Ferguson, Chris Murphy, Chris Mueller, Catherine Stockhausen, Brenndan McGuire, Colin MacKenzine, Chip Sutherland, Anne-Marie Smith, Jenn Adcock, Eric Masunaga, Laura Borealis, and for their passion course, Skippy and the folks at March.
