    Reviews for Bicycles EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Techy sounds from Italy, pls meet Elektropusher. He has already earned his name through releases on various labels and here comes his debut on Top Billin. Elektropusher aka Giovanni is a 21 years old Italian producer who took his first steps in electronic music in early 2008. His latest productions and remixes as the "18% EP" on Hervé's record label Cheap Thrills have been played and supported by some of the greatest names on the scene such as Benny Benassi, Crookers, Tommie Sunshine, Larry Tee, Kissy Sell Out, Malente, Congorock, Annie Nightingale, Boris Dlugosch, Plump Djs, The Subs to name a few. Well done young world. Bienvenuti!
