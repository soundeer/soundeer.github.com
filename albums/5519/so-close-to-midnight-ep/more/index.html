    Reviews for So Close To Midnight EP

    by Flashlights


     Back to album

    Download the So Close To Midnight Remix EP here for Free:

    kind words:

    "Blissed-out synthpop with a backbone... perfect for another year of sunny escapism." - Give Pop A Chance

    "Wow. This album might inspire about 70 spring flings and conjure up a few dozen Sagittarius children. I dare you to listen to this with your special someone while you’re cuddling up in the bed. This drop is an electronic monsoon with powers unbeknownst to most. For this to be as lyrical as it is, we rarely get to see such true pop as this, there’s usually a focus on just making the best beats they can muster up. With Flashlights and their So Close To Midnight EP, you get the best of both worlds." - Hanus TV

    "Sultry in its tone, the dark, panning layers usher you into the small hours and beyond. Like watching the city lights illuminating the air, a nighttime drive through modern day suburbia couldn't have found a better soundtrack." - 1 For The People

    "From the first synth pump of Ruby Sun to the ending notes of Sleep Alone, I was overcome with the sound, with the imagery, with the feeling that these songs bring. This is a fantastic bunch of tunes that I'm going to be cranking in my stereo (of my fly 07 civic) to and from work in the super near future!" - The Record Stache

    "The music that FLASHLIGHTS makes is infectious and with their latest ep So Close To Midnight they bring the club straight to your ears. The synths are dazzling and vibrant and along with the soft hypnotizing vocals it all flows together amazingly" - Cassette-Rewind

    "This five track EP is filled with delicious tunes that wander around sound landscapes with airy synths. I think Flashlights is doing it right with their sound because it doesn’t sound like they are trying too hard, but everything they’ve done works." - The Dankles

    "The flashy 5-track EP, appropriately titled So Close To Midnight sounds like a late-night quest for the perfect party, or at least the soundtrack to said quest." - PORTALS

    "This 5-track ep is a must have if you're into lush summer dance tunes with a touch of beach feeling. I am totally in love with this" - Eden is Neon
