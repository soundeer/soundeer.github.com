    Reviews for JOAAN - Splendor In The Grass / 115 State

    by 7even Recordings


     Back to album

    Impressive first release by French newcomer JOAAN on 7even Recordings, bringing with Splendor In The Grass and 115 State two essential cuts of production mastery.

    After hearing only a few tracks by JOAAN, label boss Greg G quickly decides to enlarge the tight 7even family to this talented producer based in Nantes, France.

    Splendor In The Grass opens the doors of a deep and emotive world, suddenly surrounded by a massive synthesizer melody exploding in a new epic drama universe. Emotional vibes.

    115 State is a real beats and track construction lesson, bringing a unique vibe between Halfstep and Dub Techno, travelling in a smoky and dubby atmosphere at 130 bpm. Real magic.

    JOAAN doesn’t only produce tracks but brings you to a real journey where you can touch your own sensations and emotions, being on a dancefloor or in the streets.

    A producer to watch very closely in the future.
