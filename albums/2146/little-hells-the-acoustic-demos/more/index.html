    Reviews for Little Hells- The Acoustic Demos

    by Marissa Nadler


     Back to album

    These are the demos recorded by me before the actual recording of the studio record. They are intimate and personal, and an interesting listen for people into that record or my more lo-fi stuff.

    If you want to get the studio album, it is available on Itunes, Amazon, etc. Also, the same goes for my album Songs III: Bird on the Water.

    released 31 December 2009 Marissa Nadler
