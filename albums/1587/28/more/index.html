    Reviews for 28

    by Virgin Blood


     Back to album

    I have been shown a lot of music on the internet. I think JANG's "28" is possibly one of the most interesting albums I have heard in some time. These songs are really interesting time wise as well as production wise and for any music fan there is something to take from 28. Experimental, amazing, from Phoenix,Arizona check it out.

    released 28 May 2011 Nick Stanifer
