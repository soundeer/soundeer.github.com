    Reviews for Moomba World Part One

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Were very proud to present our 1st Moombahton compilation. We've been fans of Moombahton since it started as one man's hobby (we see you Dave) and turned into a worldwide phenomenon. What's special to to us with Moombahton, it's its impact on the dancefloors, we've always incorporated Moombahton to our danchall / reggaeton / riddim sets and til this day we see Moombahton more as a black music with roots in island music, with a twist of modern day dance music (and those big kicks and basses that go whoomph). This compilation features excellent up and coming talent as well as already established names on the scene, with fifteen original tracks and no bootlegs. Time to get that booty bumping fam!

    released 03 January 2012
