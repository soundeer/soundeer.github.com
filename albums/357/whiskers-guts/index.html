After the release of the "Whiskers & Guts EP" in 2010, The Wind-Up Robots Killed My Cat (Luz, Christoph, Ingolf & Rouven) got the opportunety to work with Michael Hanf, who wanted to produce what is the upcoming NEW Album "Whiskers & Guts".

Five Tracks of the EP were fully new recorded, three favorites of those and three groundshaking NEW tracks were produced to form this delightful work of music.

The Album will be released in 2012, but you are able to be the first person to have the NEW Album on CD or a Limited First Print Vinyl Edition (300 copies only) at the release concert on 2nd Dec. 2011 in Würzburg, 3rd Dec. 2011 in Berlin GERMANY or any other upcoming show we will play!
