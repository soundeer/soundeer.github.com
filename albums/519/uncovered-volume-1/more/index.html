About Uncovered Volume 1

by Unwoman


 Back to album

Uncovered Volume 1 is a fan-funded covers album. This volume includes songs written between 1980 and 1995, most of which I made a special attachment to in my childhood or adolescence. Styles vary widely. I intend all songs to be tributes to the original performances without attempting to emulate them.

released 31 August 2011 Written by other people. Arranged, performed, engineered, and produced by Unwoman (Erica Mulkey). Paintings by Suzanne Rachel Forbes

Live photo by Dave Bryan

Digitally mastered by Da5id Din at Corrosive Audio

Thanks to John Ashton-Keller and Mike Estee for photographing Suzanne’s paintings.
