    Reviews for Playground Barriers

    by Virgin Blood


     Back to album

    Playground Barriers is what I believe could be a called a quintessential period record in the future. It dehydrates you and you can feel the harsh sunlight of the recordings. It's a truly powerful album that has to be listened to from start to finish. Song changes are so natural and fluid, it takes you on a journey through anxiety to only cure you with images of the future. Limited to 50 physical cassettes including photos by Angelo himself be sure to get on it!

    released 24 January 2012
