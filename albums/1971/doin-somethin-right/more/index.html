    Reviews for Doin' Somethin' Right

    by Van Atta High


     Back to album

    Doin' Somethin' Right was the band's third release. They put it out with the help of Thriving records.

    The band consisted of Stephen Kellner, Stephen Jasko, Clark Starace, Glenn Petnel, and Justin Maikisch

    released 10 March 2009 Drums by Glenn Petnel Guitar by Stephen Jasko and Justin Maikisch Bass by Clark Starace Vocals by Stephen Kellner Background Vocals by Stephen Jasko Produced by Ethan Bill Recorded, Mixed, and Mastered by Ethan Bill at Room 368 Studios
