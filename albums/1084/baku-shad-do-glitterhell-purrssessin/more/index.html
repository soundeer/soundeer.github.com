    From humble beginnings, a small community of musicians has grown, a dark, dreamlike sound has found its way first to the ears of a nation and then traveled across the world. It has become a legion of sound, without borders, without walls, without restraint. I feel lucky to be able to call many of these artists my friends. With blood, sweat, and tears so much beauty has poured forth and I've been proud to witness it evolve and change. This is for all of you and all the new listeners to come. Thank you all so much for the gift of being able to be amongst you. In addition, thank you: Toby Borrow, Mike Textbeak, Dafydd McKaharay, Ryan Knowles, James Grymmace, Joe Royster, Sean Lester, and everyone else who helped to make all this possible.

    ~ Mikey Shad-do
