    ALSO, PLEASE NOTE,

    FM is also active in doing shows in Paris and other stuff through its own collective COLLECTIF TRALALA.

    We mean by this : Collaborative and deviant structure whose purpose is to plan events, live music and performances putting forth disequilibrium, modifying and reconfigurating the lines (physical and cosmic) to interact with the stage in a more direct (/brutal?) and playful way. Our intent goes toward the reappropriation of this (ardent) zone in order to generate new relationships (between artist and audience) and situations (explosions, quaking/trembling). An attempt, perhaps, at something as protean and fragmented as ‘pop’. Our collective also takes interest in any field concerning the exploration of sound, agregating around it or having it mutate (performance, theatre, video).
