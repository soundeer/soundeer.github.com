    On November 19th, 2010 we'll be celebrating the release of "Friend Weakend" on vinyl! The night will be filled with music and eye candy. We recently teamed up with Andy Snyder of Tugboat Productions to film a video for "Sandpiper," and we're excited to feature this video that night! We invite you to join in the celebration by sharing your creativity, too! Here's what to do... I N S T R U C T I O N S : 1) Pick one of the songs above for which you'd like to create a video. (Only songs listed above are open for video submission. We'll pull songs from this list as we receive multiple submissions for one song.) 2) Email us at to inform us of your interest in making a video and to receive your free download code for "Friend Weakend." Please write your first and last name and your song of choice in the subject line...like this: "Robyn Burns-Colorfield" If you already have the album, great! We'll still respond to let you know we're pumped that you're making a video. 3) Make a video for the Action! song that you choose. (Only one video submission per person.) 4) Submit your video creation to by Midnight on Wednesday, November 10th. T H E N W H A T ? After all videos have been submitted, we'll pick our favorites. ---Chosen videos will be viewed, along with "Sandpiper," on November 19th at our Friend Weakend Vinyl Release Party @ Welcome to 1979 Studio. ---Our favorite video will also be featured on
