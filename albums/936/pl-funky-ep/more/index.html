    Reviews for Pl Funky EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Our newest release is UK Funky styled thing from…. Poland.

    Yes, may sound a bit funny but this ep definitely the best we’ve heard in Funky for a while. Our top guys from Poland, The Phantom, Teezy & Zeppy, Tears, Spox and Vanatoski bring those beats and that bass, make no mistake about it. Here’s what they got to say about the ep:

    “We’re all bedroom producers in the first place. The initial idea came about in late summer of 2009 right after coming back from this dj tour that me and Zeppy did . I knew about a few kids here and there making music, all I did was I hit them up and said “How u feelin about the idea, I think we should make this happen “. That’s the whole story – there was no key to styles, as you can feel tunes are not genre-specific – call it garage, call it grime, tropical or techno, it’s all around the shop, I left it to the crew as long as the tunes I’ve been sent stayed percussive and house tempoed. After wrapping the whole thing up we’ve sent the EP to a couple of local blogs, I remember saying no one in Europe can get this, it’s a strictly local thing for heads in Poland and let’s keep it that way. What we aimed for was building a net of young producers doing it our way, doing it different. You can find local bmore djs, you can find local house djs, you can find mashup djs in this country, you won’t hear them produce. We wanted to stay strong and go different way, simple as that. Obviously we can’t deny UK is where the inspiration mainly comes from. We doing it our way tho, without looking at the others and will keep it that way. We’re really happy seing the support Top Billin showed to our local idea, it’s a big honour for all the crew to see this EP dropping shortly." – Tears aka Thomas

    PL Funky is not a ‘genre’ tho, it’s nice and lil funny project name for this great compilation ep. We all hope you dig it, we sure do. It’s a funky ting rite now.

    released 10 May 2010
