About Something for Someone

by And the Giraffe


 Back to album

Something for Someone is the result of Nick and Josh's summer spent recording 2011.

released 21 August 2011 Most instruments played by Nick Roberts and Josh Morris. Robert Edmondson provided some awesome production, melodic bass lines and beautiful sax. Malcolm Martin played drums on Still. Vocal duties: Nick Roberts 1,2,4 Josh Morris 3,5,6 (Nick sings a little on Still) Produced: And the Giraffe Mixing: Josh Morris Album Art: Jen Pappas
