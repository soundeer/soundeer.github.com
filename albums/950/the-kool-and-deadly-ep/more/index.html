    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    A Jungle revival is brewing and DJs (thee) Mike B and Nate Day have got a lot to say about it. Their recent 90â€™s style jungle remixes as Camo UFOs for The Count and Sinden and Major Lazer have been blowing up parties worldwide and received radio play on the world renowned BBC Radio 1 platform by DJs such as Annie Mac and Toddla T.

    The pair have since been playing Festivals, Raves and world class venues all around the US with dates in Europe approaching. Official remixes are in the bag for The Glitch Mob, Mike Posner, The Hardy Boys, Nouveau Yorican, Them Jeans, Brother Reade. South Rakkas Crew and more.

    The people are ready, and these two seasoned dj/producers and their deep crates display their love for old school Jungle and Hardcore and take it back to a time when amen breaks, techno stabs, and fat bass lines ruled the dance floor while adding their own personal and modern twist to it.

    They are currently working on an EP with Sinden for his Grizzly imprint and turning out tons more original material as well as more remixes.
