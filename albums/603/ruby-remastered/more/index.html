    Reviews for Ruby (Remastered)

    by the Killjoys


     Back to album

    ARIA award winning debut for the Killjoys.

    "RUBY is heard in full on my stereo every month. The album, The Killjoys and Linda Gebar were part of one of the most enjoyable periods of my working life - I'll especially never forget the album winning an ARIA Award and Anna letting it slip to the bottom of the harbour..... But the memories aren't as important as the compositions, performance and those amazing vocals which gel together in a very unique way to form what is easily one of the top 10 Australian albums."

    Scott Murphy Managing Director MDS distributors

    Founder/Prize Director - The Australian Music Prize.

    released 01 October 2011 Produced by Mark Woods and the Killjoys
