About Endlessly

by These Hands

Melbourne instrumental midwest indie rock post rock rock shoegaze slowcore these hands


Released on 2012.

These Hands have slowly crafted their own blend of post rock, nineties indie rock, and post hardcore over a number of years. After a recent name change, many lineup changes in it's early life, tumultuous events at random intervals, and anchor dropping inertia at alot of times in between, These Hands will finally release their long awaited full length "Endlessly" in May this year.
