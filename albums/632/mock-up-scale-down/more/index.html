    Reviews for Mock Up, Scale Down

    by The Super Friendz


     Back to album

    Juno Award nominee for Best Alternative Album, 1996

    released 14 July 1995 Murderecords MURD-017 Produced by Brenndan McGuire and The Super Friendz. Recorded in a house in the north-end of Halifax December, 1994. The Super Friendz are: Drew Yamada - Guitar and vocals Charles Austin - Bass and vocals Matt Murphy - Guitar and vocals. With Dave Marsh on drums. Miniature stage built by band. Art Work by band, Catherine Stockhausen and Chris Mueller. Photos by Catherine Stockhausen. Special thanks to Colin MacKenzie, Chris Murphy, Brenndan McGuire, Jay Ferguson and Mr. & Mrs. Yamada.
