    Reviews for Yellobone EP

    by Friends of Friends Music


     Back to album

    Paul Salva has been on quite a tear as of late. In February 2011, the Frite Nite label head released his Complex Housing LP on the Friends of Friends label to rave reviews (Resident Advisor, Pitchfork, FACT, XLR8R all recommended) and has quickly become one of electronic music's most exciting and diverse producers. Since then, he's pumped out killer remixes for Rainbow Arabia, Miguel Migs, Om Unit, Beans and others all while touring relentlessly and most recently being accepted into RBMA for the Madrid sessions. With support from DJs like Night Slugs (Bok Bok, Girl Unit), Low End Theory (Gaslamp Killer, DJ Nobody), Earnest Endeavors and more, Salva is ready to continue the momentum prepping the Yellobone EP for November 15th release.

    released 15 November 2011
