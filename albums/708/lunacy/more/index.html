    Reviews for Lunacy

    by Whoa Dizzy


     Back to album

    The first Whoa Dizzy record leans more toward swinging blues-rock than later releases, but includes a few curve balls with the occasional odd time signature and some out-of-this-world excessive funk madness.

    released 14 March 2007 Nick Lutes: Lead Vocals Eric Mandt: Guitar and Backing Vocals Cheri Keller: Bass and Backing Vocals David Bacon: Drums, Keyboards, and Backing Vocals. Produced at home by Whoa Dizzy. Album recorded and mastered by David Bacon. Mixed by David Bacon and Eric Mandt.
