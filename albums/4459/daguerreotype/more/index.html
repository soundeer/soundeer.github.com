    Reviews for Daguerreotype

    by She's Electric


     Back to album

    Daguerreotype Part 1 was assembled from my "song a week" project which, as the title suggests, is a project that involves me composing, recording, and releasing a song every week. The EP series are projected to be released every 3 months and will feature what I thought were the best songs from the past quarter.

    released 01 June 2011 written by Ryo Baum performed by She's Electric mixed & recorded in Sunny California mastered by Mark Redito
