    Reviews for Brothers In Law - Gray Days EP

    by CF Records


     Back to album

    CF is excited to announce the debut release from Italy’s Brothers In Law, out February 27th 2012 on limited 7” vinyl via CF and our Italian friends at WWNBB. Fresh from supporting Girls Names on their recent Italian tour, Brothers in Law formed in 2011 when Nicola and Jack starting dating two sisters (hence the band name) and jamming with drum buddy Andrea. Inspired by JAMC, Sonic Youth, Pastels etc the three make indie pop in the classic Creation mould with cute euro-pronunciation to boot. “Gray Days” includes three songs and gets off to a flying start with the very fine “Holy Weekend”, check it out!

    released 27 February 2012
