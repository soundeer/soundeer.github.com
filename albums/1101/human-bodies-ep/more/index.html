    Reviews for Human Bodies EP

    by The French Inhales & Bottle Service


     Back to album

    4 Track EP from Austin's Love Collector. The band includes former members of The Ape Shits/The Fells/Dirty Sweets and current members of Lola Cola and The Vomettes. This is their second 7" and first official release since their appearance on Matador's "Casual Victim Pile" comp.

    released 21 April 2012 Human Bodies, You Drain My Batteries, and Non-Stop Love were recorded by Orville Neeley (Bad Sports/A Giant Dog/OBN IIIs). Circuit Breaker was recorded by Mike Vasquez at Sweat Box. Mastered by Mike Vasquez. Cover photo by Renate Winter.
