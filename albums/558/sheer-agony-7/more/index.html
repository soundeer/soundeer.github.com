    Reviews for Sheer Agony 7''

    by Sheer Agony


     Back to album

    Sheer Agony 7'' is the self-titled debut record of Montreal guitar pop trio Sheer Agony. Formed in 2010 as the recording project of Jackson MacIntosh and Play Guitar bandmate Christian Simmons, Silver Dapple bassist Markus Lake joined the group in 2011. Sheer Agony released three singles last year, "Theme from 'Tortoise And The Hare'" on Khyber Compilation II, and "Introducing Flash And The Pans" and "Caitlin and Peter and George Harrison" on Fixture Records Compilation No. 2.

    This new 8-minute album, Sheer Agony's first label release, collects some of the band's earliest recordings, done at their practice space/studio/home venue The Silver Door and mixed by guitarist/lead singer Jackson MacIntosh with Peter Woodford (of Freelove Fenner) at The Bottle Garden, Woodford's underground analog studio.

    Genre: Off-kilter late-70s guitar pop

    Recommended for fans of: The dB's, Todd Rundgren, The Soft Boys, The Homosexuals.

    Release Date: June 5, 2012
