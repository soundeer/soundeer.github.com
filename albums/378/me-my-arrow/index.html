About Me & My Arrow

by Align in Time

Washington alternative ambient film film score film soundtrack post-rock postrock rock


Released on 2011.

“Me & My Arrow is my album of the year. It's been a long time since an album resonated so much with me...it combines several revered sounds into something familiar, yet altogether different...it's beautiful, captivating, and accessible." - AbsolutePunk (93%)

“The more you listen to Me & My Arrow, the more you realize it offers something absolutely new. It is the beginning of something huge...this is post-rock 2.0, beautiful and powerful. Align in Time is one of the best discoveries of the year.” - Alternativ News (4/5)

“This album has exceeded my every expectation. This is the kind of music that easily draws you in, grabs hold of your emotions, and runs with them...every minute is fresh and pleasing to the ears. Me & My Arrow should be heard as a single entity (on repeat).” - Greg's Musik Haus (9/10)
