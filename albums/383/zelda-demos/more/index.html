    Reviews for Zelda Demos

    by Cory Johnson


     Back to album

    What you're hearing now are very rough, unmixed/unmastered demo versions of my own arrangements of Koji Kondo's music from the Legend of Zelda video game series. If you've come across this, I'd very much appreciate your time and your ears.

    Drop me a line through the contact button on
    and let me know what's good/bad about these demos. I plan on re-recording these tracks, really polishing them up, and releasing them proper later in the year. Follow me on Tumblr ( ) or Twitter ( ) to get updates and new releases.

    released 24 August 2011
