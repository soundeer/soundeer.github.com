About THE AFTER 2AM SESSIONS LP (2012)

by Ahmed Sirour


 Back to album

***UPDATE (10 June, 2012): Physical copies of this album (additionally, hand-signed & numbered by Ahmed Sirour) are now available for order at

The conclusion to 2011's release of "The After 2am Sessions EP" where up-and-coming musician/composer/producer, Ahmed Sirour, attempted an unorthodox approach by releasing a fully instrumental, slow-tempo'd, 6-track EP as his debut album, utilizing 3 themes of Love: The Romantic, the Erotic, and the Spiritual.

One year later and Ahmed returns with 6 new songs to complete the full album and release "The After 2am Sessions LP", continuing where he left off, still incorporating the same themes, but delving further to create a soundscape that completely transports the listener to another place from their sometimes mundane or chaotic reality...using music as a means to set a peaceful mood, to stimulate the senses, and to rejuvenate the soul.

This is not a mainstream album, and you may not even hear it played on most radio stations, based on its non-conformist format...this is the soothing soundtrack for every day people, supported and promoted by every day people--from all around the globe--who share a love of music and the need, at times, to just sit back and unwind.

released 15 May 2012

All music composed, arranged, performed, produced, mixed, and mastered by Ahmed Sirour (unless otherwise noted* ) *Myles Kane on electric guitar for "Warm Rain" Cleveland Jones on vocals for " Love One Another (The Vocal Re-Edit)
