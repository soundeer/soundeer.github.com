    Reviews for Dark Paradise EP

    by Gross Magic


     Back to album

    Hailing from Oxford, Mississippi, there's not much to say about ILLLS. Blending influences from Jay Reatard and The Cure, to Jeremy Jay and My Bloody Valentine, ILLLS have constructed a 6 song EP that will certainly bring even more attention to the prosperous music scene coming out of Oxford, MS at the moment.

    From the rumbling sonic assault of "Bathroom Floor" to the stadium rock guitar stabs of "Teeth", ILLLS have created something truly special with their debut Dark Paradise EP.

    Dark Paradise EP is available to pre-order on 12" and Download. All orders include two bonus tracks "Kid Dream" and "Morocco"

    released 17 June 2012
