    Reviews for Pop Massacre

    by Friends of Friends Music


     Back to album

    In an attempt to showcase some of our favorite people, Friends of Friends put out a call to a whole slew of producers. The request was simple... "choose a song from any era of popular culture to be source material of a song for our 'Pop Massacre' compilation." We're proud to say that the project (volume #1 at least) is done! All we ask of you is an email in exchange for this 13 track killing of Lady Gaga, Britney Spears, Beach Boys, Spice Girls, Mariah Carey, Katy Perry, Soulja Boy and others.

    We're excited and humbled by the awesome people who committed their time to this project and they deserve every bit you can muster up. If you like what you hear, please support the artists and labels in anyway you're capable...

    Thank you everyone for making 2010 an amazing year for the FoF Network of artists and labels and lookout for an even better '11...enjoy!

    released 13 December 2010 Special Thanks to all the artists involved
