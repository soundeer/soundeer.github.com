    Reviews for Jack Box

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    In techno we trust. Sir Nenis is the founding father and main producer of Top Billin, as well as one half of the heavy-bass producers Sharkslayer. Now he has teamed up with his cousin for this release of 2 tech sounding house tracks. These both are crafted for the dancefloor with lots of bass and twisted soundscapes. Sir Nenis and Belmont have been both working on music for a time now but this is their first ever release together. Hope you enjoy this blast of techy sounds and big room vibes.

    released 05 August 2011
