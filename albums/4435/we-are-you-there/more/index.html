    Reviews for We Are You There

    by Rose For Bohdan


     Back to album

    31 song collection of all releases by the 90s bay area California band, rRope. All tracks remastered. Includes both albums, all 7"s, and previously unreleased live tracks from their last show, opening for Sonic Youth.

    released 15 May 2012 Tracks 1-11 from self-titled cd, originally released on Smilex (Feb 1995) Tracks 12-17 from Mahagonny cd, originally released on Silly Bird (1998) Tracks 18-20 from West Tone Songs 7", originally released on Fear of Pop Music (1993) Track 21 from split 7" with VNC, originally released on Brickfactory (Sept 1994) Track 22 from Music For Swingers 12" compilation, originally released on Massacre at Central High (1995) Tracks 23-26 from A Thing of Beauty is a Joy Forever 2x7", originally released on Two Staw (1996) Tracks 27-31 Previously unreleased All tracks remastered by Jeff Lipton at Peerless Mastering, Boston, MA Assistant engineer: Maria Rice Tracks 1-17 recorded by Greg Freeman, Lowdown Studios Tracks 18-20 recorded by Dan Rathbun, Polymorph Studios Track 21 recorded on boombox, Turk Street; Dave Pankenier on drums Track 22 recorded by Scott Thiessen, Warepad Tracks 23-25 recorded by BART, Guerilla Euphonics Track 26 recorded at Secret Studios Tracks 27-31 recorded live at the SF Fillmore, May 27, 1998.
