    Reviews for Beyond Good and Evil

    by Third Realm


     Back to album

    Blending elements of Industrial Rock, Darkwave & EBM music, Third Realm returns with their fifth full length album. When listening to a Third Realm album, you will notice a diverse mixture of songs, Beyond Good and Evil continues this trend, combining various influences and styles resulting in a unique; well rounded release. A conceptual representation of good and evil is defined by the ever changing moods this release portrays. Remixer/Producer Justin Lassen (Nine Inch Nails, Linkin Park, Evanescence) gives us an outstanding remix of Lighthouse, taking the track to a whole new level. Whether you prefer the aggressive side of Third Realm, or the morbid darkwave chantings of this project, you will find familiar comfort in these tracks.
