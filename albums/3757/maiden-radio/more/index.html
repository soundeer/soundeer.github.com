    Reviews for Maiden Radio

    by Maiden Radio


     Back to album

    This album was recorded on May 3rd and 4th of 2010. All songs have unknown authors, with the following exceptions. “Wildwood Flower” was originally titled “I’ll Twine ‘Mid the Ringlets,” written by Maud Irving (words) and Joseph P. Webster (music). It is dedicated to Mrs. Bessie Mize. “Weary Blues” was originally titled “Weary Blues from Waitin’” and written by Hank Williams, Sr. (Sony/ATV/Acuff Music). "The Reason Why" is a Maiden Radio original.

    Thank you Kevin Ratterman for capturing these recordings in The Kitchen at The Funeral Home. Also Daniel Martin Moore for photographing the session and whistling on “Weary Blues.” Album art by Christopher Jackson.

    Put a bonnet on it.

    released 26 September 2010
