    by Seapony

    Side A - Sailing

    Side B - I Saw You

    The first new material since their debut LP, Go With Me, was released earlier this year. "Sailing" pushes the Seapony sound into new territory, featuring xylophone, syncopated hand-claps, and acoustic guitar, all layered over a wash of memorable guitar melodies.
