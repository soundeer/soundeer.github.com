    Reviews for Migration Routes EP (CNVX002)

    by Gherat


     Back to album

    Migration Routes EP is Convex Collective's second release (CNVX002) as Convex Records. Migration Routes EP has been crafted by our number one European artist. Gherat, a producer/visual artist from Amsterdam, he has a promising year ahead.

    The production of this EP is undeniably intricate and with echoes of sound reminiscent of Flying Lotus and early Rephlex material, Migration Routes contains some of the most rich and colourful sounds we have heard to date.
