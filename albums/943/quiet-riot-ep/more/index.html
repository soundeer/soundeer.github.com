    Reviews for Quiet Riot EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Manaré (aka Riot Kid) is our cousin from Paris and is undoubtedly one of the most interesting producers of the new wave of French artists. And here comes his first ep on Top Billin! Here’s what they say about him:

    He caused riots as a teenager. Now that he’s (almost) an adult, he causes wars. This descendant of a family of troublemakers carries on with the traditions and honors his ancestors. As an entertainer at heart, the kid from Paris wants it all.

    After a very successful first E.P, that was played on Radio 1, in every club and in your girlfriend’s iPod and in which he glorified the Orient and Wes Anderson, Riot Kid is now Manaré and he’s back to tell you about horns, jazz clubs and Ecuadorian rivers.”

    Check his amazingly dope ep with influences from tech house, Uk funky, electro and bass music. Remixes come from none other than L-VIS 1990, FRENCH FRIES & BAMBOUNOU, FEMME EN FOURRURE and MERK MENY. Definitely a release not to sleep on!

    released 06 August 2010
