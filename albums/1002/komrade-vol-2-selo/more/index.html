    Reviews for Komrade Vol. 2: Selo

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    We’re happy to welcome our second Russian release on the label (on our ‘Komrade’ –series) this time from Moscow wonderboy Roby Howler. Currently releasing tracks on forward thinking labels like Top Billin, Gash Digital and Globelle, Roby’s productions are striking a chord across Europe and beyond with taste-maker djs. He has also just remixed ‘Springer’ by Crookers/Neoteric/Wax Motif - Dr Gonzo that will out on Southern Fried. Roby’s musical taste goes from deep bass tracks to high-powered house, so you can expect almost anything from him in the future, just the way we like it.

    released 23 September 2011
