    Reviews for Sounds Of Your Lovers - Single

    by Dozens


     Back to album

    Vinyl 7":

    A side: "Sounds Of Your Lovers"

    B side: "Birthday"

    Press Quotes:

    "Indie pop at its best, extremely catchy" - Indie Track of the Day

    "Sounds Of Your Lovers is a melodic masterpiece...the sound of electric guitars fuses with an up-beat piano riff to provide a fitting backdrop for an excellent chorus." - FMV Magazine (UK)

    “Sounds Of Your Lovers has been on repeat all weekend – on the laptop, in the car, or wherever else we were able to plug the iPod in ... this is an exuberant, arms aloft – and ultimately summery – noise...it’ll leave an indelible mark in your consciousness after just one listen, so insistent is its chorus." - Laissez Faire Club (UK)

    "Dozens unleash a barrage of sweet, sweet harmonies on "Sounds Of Your Lovers", which is an arena ready, power-pop singalong. From the reverse-waterfall keyboard at the song's start to the ELO-esque guitars that propel the chorus, the song is a fun one." - Loud Loop Press

    "If you’ve ever wanted to hear what a chillwave re-imagining of a song by The Sugarcubes would sound like, look no further. Dozens keep it fairly close to the original on "Birthday", but instead of jangle pop guitars and Bjork’s wonderfully exuberant vocals, you get neon synths, handclap beats, and sleepy-time vocals...it all works really well...I actually really like the cover." - Perfect Midnight World

    "Dozens have a stellar 7” single called “Sounds Of Your Lovers”...The a-side is a great original track called “Sounds Of Your Lovers” while the b-side is a f*cking wonderful cover of Sugarcubes’ “Birthday.” I mean the original is just Bjork-y brilliance, but this cover is just out of this world. Washed Out meets Animal Collective-esque amazing. Did I mention it’s amazing lol? Seriously, I’m in love." - Dance Yrself Clean
