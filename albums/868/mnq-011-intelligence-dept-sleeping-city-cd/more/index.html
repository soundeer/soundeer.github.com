    Reviews for MNQ 011 Intelligence Dept. - Sleeping City CD

    by Mannequin Label + Mailorder


     Back to album

    The second collaboration between Mannequin and Anna Logue Records is the top-notch Minimal Electro Wave band INTELLIGENCE DEPT., hailing from Ferrara, Italy.

    The release includes both their complete and impossible-to-get demo cassettes “Sleeping City” (1984) and “The Big Trouble” (1985), thus also including their well-known beautiful tracks “Sleeping City”, “Anger Inside”, “Too Late To Love” which were also featured on the 1984 LP compilation “A White Chance”, and “Loneliness” from Mannequin’s “Danza Meccanica – Italian Synth Wave 1982-1987” LP/CD compilation, as well as a cover version of “Sister Europe”, originally by The Psychedelic Furs.

    Additionally, the CD version includes four live tracks, “Sleeping City” and “Too Late To Love”, as well as the two previously totally unknown tracks “Nice Faces” and “Black Widow”, recorded live at the Arena Nuovo in Ferrara in 1985, so a total of 13 amazing tracks, beautifully presented in a six-panel digipak.

    • high quality manufacturing made in Germany

    • limited edition of 500 copies

    • six-panel digipak

    released 28 March 2011
