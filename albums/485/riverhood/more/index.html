About Riverhood

by Holobody


 Back to album

Crack in the Road: "...there are few artists who succeed in capturing the essence of idyllic songwriting in as magical a method as the Loseth siblings."

Gigseen: "The obvious talents of both compose some great, understated and brilliantly succinct moments of poppiness (without having to sling a blatant hook in the face), and the result is astonishingly competent and engrossing."

released 22 May 2012 Luke Loseth/Felix Green - music & lyrics, vox, guitar, percussion, piano, synth, bells Charlotte "Sea" Oleena - music & lyrics, vox Jennifer Loseth - violin, additional vox Alex Scott, Mike Hansen, Fiona Loseth, Derek Loseth - additional vox Mixed and produced by Luke Loseth
