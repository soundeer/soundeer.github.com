    Reviews for Summer Twins

    by Summer Twins


     Back to album

    released 24 January 2012 All songs & lyrics written by Chelsea Brown. All arrangements written by the Summer Twins: Chelsea Brown- guitar, vocals Justine Brown- drums, vocals Danny Delgado- bass Marcio Rivera- guitar "I Could Never Break Your Heart" features guest vocalist Cameron Thorne of Naive Thieves. Guitar solo in "Pickin' Daisies" was written by John Dust. All organ by John Dust. Recorded analog at Unisex Studios in Los Angeles, CA June-July 2011. Recorded, produced, & mixed by John Dust & Don Bolles. Mastered by Patrick Haight. Cover photos by Joy Newell.

    Artwork by Chelsea Brown. For mom & dad. We couldn't have made this album without your encouragement & support. Also special thanks to: Don & Jack, Sean & Lee at Burger Records, Joy Newell, Cameron Thorne, & IRMA Records.
