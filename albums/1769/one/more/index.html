    Reviews for One

    by Kumon Plaza


     Back to album

    This is the original soundtrack for a short film by Bryce Evans called "one".

    "one is an insight into one teen's loneliness during the periods of high school and post-secondary education; a time where he finds himself, and the key to breaking out of the lonely mindset. It helps to solidify the ideas from the photos and put them into the perspective of the life of one character. An inspiring story of how to overcome loneliness to find your passion and follow your dreams."
