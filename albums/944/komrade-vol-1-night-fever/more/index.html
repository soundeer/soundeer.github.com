    Reviews for Komrade vol. 1: Night Fever

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    What do you know about Russia? Not much, we think. But Russia is a great country with a burgeoning electronic music scene and as an international label, Top Billin are proud to present our first signing from that huge and distant country.

    Enter GIANT from St. Petersburg, 3 guys who make everything from dubstep (they’ve released stuff previously on Hench and Dub Police) to house and beyond. So here’s 2 tracks of high-fuelled and bass heavy house with a tech twist from our favourite Russians Giant. These tunes bang hard.

    As we’ve been to Russia to party many times and we know how hard they can go so it’s suitable to have this release on Top Billin. United we stand, divided we fall.

    released 27 August 2010 Written and produced by Giant
