    Biography

    Two girls from Amsterdam who travelled the world together, lived in a house without tv and internet for a while and then decided to form a band together. They make singer/songwriteresque pop with dreamy beats like Nick Drake or Bon Iver would make if they would have lived in the 60s and would have been girls.
