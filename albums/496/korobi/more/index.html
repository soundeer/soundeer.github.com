    Reviews for KOROBI

    by L.A.S.'s Crime


     Back to album

    "....shiro (white) geisha were purely entertainers, as opposed to korobi geisha, who 'tumbled' for guests: kido (gate) geisha stood at the entrances to carnivals, playing their shamisens to attract business, whereas joro (whore) geisha were probably not hired for their musical skills...."

    Original 1989 demo digitally remastered on 2012.

    Each song is introduced by a short instrumental atmosphere.

    The demo is intended for a continuous play, without breaks among tracks (not possible in this online preview).

    Recorded on tape in Italy on January '89 with a 4 tracks Tascam Portaone, Korobi is entirely made of:

    • Sandro Marchetti (voice & hands)

    • Maria Elena Brogi (voice & hands)

    • Roland tr 606 (drums)

    • Roland sh101 (synth)

    • Yamaha rx11 (drums)

    • Yamaha rm10 (drums)

    • Yamaha cs01 (synth)

    • Yamaha rx50 (multi effector)

    • Korg poly 800 (synth)

    • Akai s612 (sampler)

    • Simmons sds202 (drums)

    • Casio sk1 (sampler)

    • Casio vl1 (synth)

    • Boss dsd2 digital sampler stompbox (sampler)

    • Ken Multi delay (multi effector)

    On 2012 the original tape has been digitized at 96khz 32bit. Selected tracks from Korobi will be reissued on vinyl by Mannequin on September 2012.

    ATTENTION! The audio quality of this demo is not comparable with a professional studio production, so listen carefully to free online previews before buying.

    released 12 January 1989 thanks to Alessandro Adriani of Mannequin
