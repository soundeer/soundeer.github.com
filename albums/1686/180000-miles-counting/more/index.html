    Reviews for 180​.​000 Miles & Counting

    by The Dynamics


     Back to album

    4 years after the international success of their 1st album “Versions Excursions”, The Dynamics present their long awaited sophomore effort. More personal and imagined as the travel book of their years of intense touring over the world, “180.000 Miles & Counting” is just as simple, genuine, curious and joyful as the cool bunch of passionate musicians who gave birth to it.

    FEEDBACKS:

    • Laurent Garnier: "Excellent!"

    • Simon Harrison (Basic Soul Radio): "This is one album for soaking up the sunhine - ACE"

    • Jeremy Sole (KCRW): "More fiyah from Dynamics... always a sureshot, indeed!"
