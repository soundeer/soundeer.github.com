    Reviews for IN LUNGS

    by LIQUID SKULLS


     Back to album

    songs were recorded between the months of may and july with one focused week amid the latter month..

    almost all long distance, aside from that latter week.

    mostly, an acoustic guitar was used and recorded with a built-in laptop mic... most vocals too..

    various software was used to make beats and some synths.

    an analog synth was used more.

    an electric guitar was used for one part burried in there somewhere.

    various samples were used.

    drums and a tamborine were hit.

    headphones were used.. you should use them too.

    released 06 August 2011 written, performed, recorded, and mixed by liquid skulls jimmy spice - guitar/beats/synth jack lloyd - vocals/synth/beats/percussion/guitar expanded from solo recordings by jimmy spice alone in a bedroom with a laptop and guitar mastered by mikal evan childers konnected contains samples created by dobriode and kaponja atoms contains a sample created by halleck found at and used under creative commons license design by

    big thanks to kale ogle, neil lord, joe at maba tapes, mikal evan childers, tim green, travis mcelroy, the lloyds, the spices. in memory of lucas clayton hunsicker and dante jacuzzi...
