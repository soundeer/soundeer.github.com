    Reviews for With You

    by Dreams


     Back to album

    Dreams is very proud to release our first two tracks ever! We've worked very hard on them and hope you enjoy them to the fullest. They are part of a larger album set to be released at the beginning of 2012. Feel free to donate as much or as little as you like, or nothing at all. Above all, we want you to hear it!

    released 29 September 2011 Robert Shelton - Keyboards Emily Ritz - Vocals Jake Nochimow - Drums Andrew Maguire - Vibraphone, Marrimba Geneva Harrison - Percussion Jesse Cafiero - Bass, Lap Steel Guitar Zach Rubin-Rattet - Electric Guitar with Very Special Guests Thao Nguyen - Background Vocals on "With You" Jeremy Robert Harris - Acoustic Guitar on "Don't Want This to Die" Nathan Blaz - Cello on "Don't Want This to Die" Produced by Robert Shelton and James Riotto Engineered and Mixed by James Riotto at Tiny Telephone Studios, San Fransisco Mastered by JJ Golden Dreams would like to thank John Vanderslice and Matthew Colecchi for helping out in so many ways.
