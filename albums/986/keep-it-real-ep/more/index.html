    Reviews for Keep It Real EP

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Johannes & Jonatan, more known as dj/producer duo Woo Banger has for the last couple of years hosted, arranged and played a series of successful club nights in their hometown of Malmö, Sweden, as well as playing everywhere from illegal after hours clubs to high profile venues. When dancing to WooBanger DJ-ing, crowds can expect sets containing everything from deeper Berlin house to fun filled bangers, via rhythmic tech, always presented with tremendous joy and an intense stage presence. As producers on their debut ep for Top Billin you can except something similar - house in a broader sense. From banging 90's vibeing diva vocals in "Back to the rave" and "I know", to the rolling fun filled tech house in "Holy birds" and hard hitting ghetto-pastiche "Keep it real", all tracks have one thing in common - they are made for the floor.

    released 15 July 2011
