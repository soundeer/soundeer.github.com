    Reviews for Flick It Up

    by So Shifty


     Back to album

    Say hello to Swick! He's another one of these obscenely young & talented Australian DJ/Producer types. Already signed to "Sweat It Out" label for his super fun track "Grow Up" as well as for Malente’s No Brainer label for his tropical “Jogalani” ep, this young cat is making MOVES. Swick sounds like a fun youth, messing with lego, playing battle toads on your NES and just kicking it in blissful ignorance. You should definitely keep an eye or an ear out for Swick in the future. Remixes come from such great names as Will Bailey, Lucid and Myd.

    released 26 April 2011 Written and produced by Swick & Marsepan
