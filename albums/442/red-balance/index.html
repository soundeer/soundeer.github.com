About Red Balance

by Anna Morley

Barcelona ambient electro-acoustic film music instrumental vibraphone synth lounge slowcore soundtrack


Anna reunites with UK based producer Alex Forster to create Red Balance- a fresh collection of electro-acoustic elements, incorporating a broad tapestry of live and electronic textures. Morley and Forster collaborated on the album over a period of almost 2 years between Barcelona and London to form a unique blend of new sounds, making Red Balance in a class of it's own.
