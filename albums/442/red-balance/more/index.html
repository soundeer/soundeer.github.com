About Red Balance

by Anna Morley


 Back to album

Anna reunites with UK based producer Alex Forster to create Red Balance- a fresh collection of electro-acoustic elements, incorporating a broad tapestry of live and electronic textures. Morley and Forster collaborated on the album over a period of almost 2 years between Barcelona and London to form a unique blend of new sounds, making Red Balance in a class of it's own.

released 22 July 2011 All compositions by Anna Morley © 2011 except "24-25" by Erlend Øye and Eirik Glambek Bøe (EMI) and "Spanish Armada" by Mike Stroud and Evan Mast (XL Recordings) Anna Morley: vibraphone, violin, melodica, vocals, percussion, glockenspiel, keys Alex Forster: synth, guitars, percussion Moomaw Jackson: ukulele Tom Ansink: pianos
