    Bio:

    Art Imperial has never surfed in real life. The waves he chases are those crashing on the shores of his dreams. There is no stretch of sand in the suburbs to lay, no body of water to immerse the soul, no glittering reflections of an orange sunset over a crystal blue blanket. The Surf Suburban EP is a dream: a sonorous expression of the sea’s seduction. It overflows with the raw energies of the sun, the sand the surf; the forces that cleanse and renew. The purpose: to incite and affirm the undying pulse of life that ecstatically attacks the senses, transporting us to worlds not normally felt and experienced. Art Imperial has never surfed in real life, but everyday he chases those waves.
