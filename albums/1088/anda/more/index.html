    Dark Mother makes dream pop out of nightmares. Crass and corrosive, yet hypnotic at the same time; the duo fit in perfectly with the musical boundaries pushed by groups like Crystal Castles and CRIM3S. There's definitely something else going on here though. Trap infused snare rolls and a voice at times possessed by Julee Cruise and at other times unleashing an inner banshee, Dark Mother manage to carve their own signature in an arena congested with imitation.
