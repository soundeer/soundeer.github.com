    Reviews for 54, I love you but it's time to get off

    by Simone Morelli


     Back to album

    Horrible Present is the solo project of the London-based Italian musician Nicola Donà, whose love for the classics from the good old 50s is channeled through short, psychedelic pop songs.

    Side A includes his latest 3 song EP "54" - including the highly addictive tune "The Girl Who Found the World" - plus two new songs, the new single "Classics at Night" and "Heaven on Earth".

    The side B includes... three b-sides, "Spiral Stairs", "Stuck Like No Clue" and "Pale. Haze. Space. Race." plus a remix of an old song, Damaged Droplets, by Welcome Back Sailors. There's also a digitally exclusive bonus track for those who download it.
