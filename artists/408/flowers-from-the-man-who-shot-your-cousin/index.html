    From France.

    In French, the Magician or Juggler is called Bateleur, meaning the swindler or sleight of hand artist, and a practitioner of stage magic. The Italian tradition calls him Il Bagatello, which has similar connotations to the French. Also known as the Artisan, in the Mantegna Tarocchi, he is the second lowest in the series, outranking only the Beggar.
