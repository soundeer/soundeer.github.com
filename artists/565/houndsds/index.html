HOUNDSds

Brooklyn  brooklyn  dream pop  guitar pop  indie pop  jangle pop  pop 

    HOUNDSds was born in late 2009 as a recording project of part-time magician and cartoon tree arboriculturist, Patrick McIntyre. His only goals were to produce lush, dreamy pop songs, and find a cure for bovine herpes. A string of self-released digital-only singles and mini-albums followed, including The Technology of Love (2010), Los Dos (2011), and the critically acclaimed Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Heart's Club Band (1967). In 2012, HOUNDSds' first physical release, Phone Thou, came out on cassette via Chill Mega Chill Records. Presently, McIntyre is working on morphing HOUNDSds from a recording-only entity into a performing band dedicated to proselytizing the gospel of Tony (apocryphal).
