Paper Cages

Colchester  alternative  alternative rock  folk  folk rock  guitar pop  guitar rock  indie  indie rock  pop punk  pop rock  punk rock  rock  synth 

    Paper Cages formed in early 2011. After recording our first demo at the start of July 2011, we decided it was time to get out there. Starting with local venues and bars, moving up to larger performances at Universities, and the upcoming tour and debut Album release in 2012.

    We are five friends from Colchester with a lot of drive and ambition. Our dynamic and energetic form of pop rock, draws punk and indie influences, forming something raw, original and honest.
